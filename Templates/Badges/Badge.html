Badges

Badges are used to provide additional information regarding the content, for example, in the mail icon, a numbered badge may be used to signify the number of unread emails, or in social media sites, a green badge usually signifies the online status of the person.

Icon Badge

These are badges on icons, which are usually seen in places such as mail or cart(e-commerce). You can use classes like .badgeTopRight and .badgeTopLeft for respective positions.

Badge on Avatars

These are badges on avatars, which generally describe the status of the user. You can use classes like .badgeRed, .badgeYellowand .badgeGreen for respective colors and statuses of the badges.

BorderBadges

The borders which change color or provide information regarding the status of the icon or user are called border badges. You can use a wrapper class .borderBadge and then a color class to apply a border badge on that avatar.
