Ratings

Ratings provide insight regarding others' opinions and experiences, and can allow the user to submit a rating of their own.

Basic Ratings

These are the basic rating components. They rely on the solid variant available on the font-awesome library to switch to the checked version.

Read only Ratings

These are Read only ratings, The user's response is not needed here. It's merely there as a part of a showcase or for information.

Sizes

These are the basic rating components. You can change the size of the icons by changing the font size.
